About Me

As an ambitious and innovative bioengineer, I’m currently pursuing a Master of Science in Bioengineering, leveraging a strong foundation gained through my Bachelor's degree from the University of Colorado. My expertise lies in the intersection of medical devices, 3D modeling, AI, and user-centered design, with hands-on experience in cutting-edge research and real-world healthcare solutions.

I’ve led and contributed to groundbreaking projects, such as developing a voice-activated medical bed that enhanced independence for a quadriplegic patient and designing synchronized sensor systems for wheelchair mobility tracking. These initiatives not only addressed technical challenges but also improved patient care and usability, showcasing my ability to deliver impactful, user-focused solutions. My experience navigating the complexities of regulatory compliance, COMIRB approval, and collaborative cross-functional environments speaks to my ability to execute projects from concept to completion.

Known for my analytical thinking, project management expertise, and commitment to solving healthcare challenges, I thrive in fast-paced environments where innovation is key. I bring a global perspective, being fluent in English and proficient in French and Hindi, and a passion for advancing bioengineering to improve patient outcomes and push the boundaries of medical technology.

With a commitment to continuous learning and a passion for improving lives through technology, I am excited to contribute to the future of bioengineering and medical innovation.
